Frequently Asked Questions

How long is the ConnectSIM card validity?

Flexiroam ConnectSIM card has a lifetime validity. It doesn’t expire.

Can I reuse the ConnectSIM card?

Yes, Flexiroam ConnectSIM can be used for multiple trips. Get one and use it on all your trips. All you need is an active data plan in your Flexiroam X App account.

How can I top-up or recharge the SIM card?

Data top-up is easy. Proceed to your Flexiroam X App, tap GET DATA PLAN > select a DATA PLAN > CONFIRM ORDER > PAY with Wallet credit or Credit/Debit card.

Will the ConnectSIM be compatible with my mobile phone/Device?

Flexiroam ConnectSIM card is compatible with all phone models and mobile devices as long as the device is unlocked.

What is the SIM card size?

ConnectSIM card is a 3-in-1 SIM(Nano, Micro, and Regular size) which will work with all phones and devices.

Will the ConnectSIM card work on a portable WiFi device?

Yes, Flexiroam ConnectSIM works fine on any kind of portable WiFi device as long as the device is unlocked.

If my phone is carrier locked, can I use the ConnectSIM?

No, ConnectSIM is compatible with unlocked devices ONLY. Please unlock your device before using Flexiroam ConnectSIM.

Can I use the ConnectSIM in a dual SIM phone?

Yes, ConnectSIM works well with dual SIM phones. It is highly advisable to use the SIM in SIM 1 slot or in your default data/internet SIM card slot.

What happens if I lost my ConnectSIM?

Don’t worry, you can get a free ConnectSIM replacement if you lost your SIM. However, delivery charges apply.

How do I get a replacement SIM?

Use the In-App support or contact support@flexiroam.com to request for replacement. Your replacement microchip will be delivered to your doorstep within 7-21 business days depending on your delivery address and location. Shipping charges apply.

Can I use more than one ConnectSIM?

No, each passenger is only entitled one ConnectSIM. If you received more than one ConnectSIM, you may gift it to a friend or family.

Where can I use my ConnectSIM?

The ConnectSIM can be used in over 150 countries. Please check the coverage above.

Why should I link my ConnectSIM in the Flexiroam X app?

To check your data balance and manage your account. In addition, you can reuse your ConnectSIM easily for future trips after you link your ConnectSIM in the Flexiroam X App.

How long does my ConnectSIM plan last?

The ConnectSIM plan is valid for 1 year from the date of connection. However, once you exhausted the data in the SIM, you will need to purchase additional plans to use the SIM.

Can I receive One-Time Password (OTP) SMS for online banking purposes?

No, you will not be able to receive SMS with ConnectSIM. To receive SMS for banking purposes (OTP), you will need to insert your Home SIM.

Can I make normal voice calls using ConnectSIM (Domestic/IDD) ?

No, you will not be able to make or receive calls & SMS with ConnectSIM. ConnectSIM provides data only and you may call using any application that uses Voice Over Internet Protocol (VOIP) such as WhatsApp Call, Viber Call, WeChat Call and etc. However, you may purchase Global Data Plan to make normal voice calls via Flexiroam X App.

What happens after I finished the 100MB pre-loaded data?

Once the 100MB is fully utilized, you will need to purchase additional plans to use the SIM. To top-up, please proceed to your Flexiroam X App, tap GET DATA PLAN > select a DATA PLAN > CONFIRM ORDER > PAY with Wallet credit or Credit/Debit card.

Can I purchase ConnectSIM?

No, ConnectSIM is not for sale. It is only available for Malaysia Airlines passengers for selected international flights.
